Phase One Artists
=================

This is a list of artists that may be associated with Phase One Network and are thus likely to issue copyright strikes. Note that in some cases only some of their work may be protected by this firm, and they may have different individual policies on what they do and don't permit. Definitely at least check on the fate of other songs by these artists before making any of your own. `Karaoke Nerds <https://www.karaokenerds.com>`_ search is good at showing when versions of tracks were created but no longer available.
